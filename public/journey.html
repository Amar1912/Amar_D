About Me

My Journey

From curiosity to craft — the path that shaped who I am as a developer and designer.

Hello

I'm Alex Morgan

I'm a developer passionate about crafting accessible, pixel-perfect user interfaces that blend thoughtful design with robust engineering. My favorite work lies at the intersection of design and development, creating experiences that not only look great but are meticulously built for performance and usability.

Currently based in San Francisco, I've had the opportunity to develop software across a variety of settings — from advertising agencies and large corporations to start-ups and digital product studios.

In my spare time, I'm usually exploring new technologies, contributing to open-source projects, or enjoying a good cup of coffee while sketching out new ideas. I believe in continuous learning and pushing the boundaries of what's possible on the web.

The Path So Far

A timeline of my professional growth, from first lines of code to building impactful digital experiences.

2018 — The Beginning

Discovering the Craft

Started my journey with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. Built my first websites and discovered a passion for creating things people could interact with. Enrolled in a computer science program and spent countless nights exploring web development tutorials.

2019 — First Steps

Junior Developer

Landed my first role at a small digital agency. Worked on client projects, learned to collaborate with designers, and gained hands-on experience with React and modern CSS. Built over 15 websites and started understanding the business side of development.

2020 — Growth

Frontend Developer

Moved to a tech startup where I honed my skills in building scalable applications. Became proficient in TypeScript, state management, and component architecture. Led the frontend development of a SaaS product that grew to 10,000+ users.

2021 — Expansion

Full Stack Developer

Expanded into backend development, learning Node.js, databases, and cloud infrastructure. Started taking on more responsibility, mentoring junior developers, and contributing to architectural decisions. Became passionate about performance optimization.

2023 — Leadership

Senior Developer

Joined a leading product studio as a senior developer. Led technical initiatives, established coding standards, and helped build products for Fortune 500 clients. Started speaking at meetups and writing about web development.

2024 — Present

Independent Creative Developer

Now working independently, crafting premium digital experiences for select clients. Focusing on innovative projects that push creative and technical boundaries. Building products that make a difference while maintaining the joy of creation.
